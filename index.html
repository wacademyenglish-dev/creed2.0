Track 12 • Grammar Rules

Rule 1: Short Words

For short words, we add THE + -EST.

Old The Oldest
Fast The Fastest

Track 13 • Grammar Rules

Rule 2: Long Words

For long words (3+ syllables), we use THE MOST.

Beautiful The Most Beautiful
Expensive The Most Expensive

Track 14 • Grammar Rules

Rule 3: The Rebels

These words change completely. You must memorize them.

Good The Best
Bad The Worst
